Part 1. How to Download Netflix Movies on MacBook

Netflix is always your top choice when it comes to the top streaming service to watch movies, TV series or shows. It creates countless classic originals, from House of Cards, Black Mirror to Squid Game, Arcane. The question is - when you’re in a 5-hour plane trip or long time road trip towards your summer vacation destination, you prefer to watching Netflix movies or TV series on a bigger screen for better visual experience and eye friendness. Your MacBook is superior to your iPhone iPad at this point.

Well, another question comes. Technically, you can’t directly download Netflix movies on your MacBook as there is no Netflix app for Mac version and the Netflix website doesn’t support downloading functionality that you can find on your iPhone and iPad.

In such case, you have another 3 ways to download movies from Netflix on Mac for later offline watching.

Method 1. Stream Netflix Movies from iPhone iPad to MacBook via AirPlay

AirPlay feature is natively supported by all Apple devices, from iOS iPhone iPad to macOS MacBook Pro or Air. Plus, Netflix app supports AirPlay, as well. Check the how-to guide if you have no idea about how to airplay.

Step 1. Make sure AirPlay feature is on. Generally, AirPlay feature is defaultly open on iPhone iPad, MacBook and Netflix app. If not, go to Settings to turn it on manually.

Step 2. Open the Netflix app on your iPhone or iPad > find the desired movie, TV series or show you wanna download and download them on your iOS devices.

Note: the default setting of Netflix app for iPhone is downloading movies only in Wi-Fi circumstance. If you lack strong Wi-Fi connection or have no Wi-Fi, you need to turn it off in Netflix app settings first before you download Netflix movies to iPhone.

Step 3. Go to My Downloads folder and tap your downloaded file to play it. Meanwhile, use AirPlay feature to real-time stream Netflix content from iPhone iPad screen to your MacBook bigger screen for offline watching.

In case you don’t plan to use both iPhone and MacBook for a movie watching, the second option is suitable for you. Netflix has Netflix app for Windows PC and you can have both macOS and Windows PC operating systems on your MacBook once you have Boot Camp on your MacBook. Read more details.

Method 2. Download Movies Using Netflix Windows App on MacBook via Boot Camp

Boot Camp is software developed by Apple, aiming to let you use both the macOS and Microsoft Window on your MacBook at the same time. When you need downloading Netflix movies on Macbook laptop, you just swift your operating system from macOS to Windows PC. Then, you can run Netflix app on your Windows operating system on MacBook and then download Netflix movies, TV series or TV shows to MacBook without further ado.

Method 3. Download Netflix Movies on MacBook via QuickTime Player

Don’t forget your MacBook built-in QuickTime player, which comes loaded with a Mac screen recording feature to screen capture Netflix movies on MacBook with nary an issue. More importantly, you don’t have to install anything or add any plug-in. Just follow the below steps to screen record Netflix full movie on Macbook (if you have no experience):

Step 1. Launch QuickTime Player on your Mac.

Step 2. Hit File on the menu bar at the left side of your Mac screen > select New Screen Recording from the drop-down menu.

Step 3. Click the caret next to the red record button to pick your audio options.

Step 4. Choose recording Netflix movie partially or wholly. For full screen capture, you can click anywhere on your screen and then click the prompt. To record a part of the screen, just drag the cursor from one corner of the section to the other, and then click record when prompt.

Note: QuickTime records Mac screen video footage at 30fps frame rates by default. In case higher frame rates like 60fps is what you need, please click on Apple menu icon at the upper left corner of your Mac > select System Preferences > Display > Display tab > tick the Scaled option > select Larger Text > tap OK. Then, your Netflix screen recording will be captured at 60FPS (larger in size, better in visual effects).

Step 5. Click on the red record button to begin capturing Netflix streaming movie you are playing on MacBook.

Step 6. After recording the section you need, just click the Stop button in the Menu bar on the right side of your Mac screen. When the recording is finished, your Netflix recorded file will be auto saved in .mov format, playable in Quicktime player.

Note: Not all of the Netflix movies are downloadable even though you’ve subscribed to Netflix app. In such case, your QuickTime screen recording might capture black screen if Netflix movie is DRM protected. You need to check the Netflix movie property first.

Please be noted that there are still a large number of movies and TV shows undownloadable on Netflix even though you’ve subscribed to Netflix app. If you can’t download movies from Netflix app, you can try other methods to download movies to MacBook. Keep in mind that downloading legal movies on MacBook for legal personal use and that abide by your local copyright protection law.

Part 2. How to Download Disney+ Movies on Mac

Can you download Disney plus movies on Mac? No, there is currently no official way to download Disney+ movies directly on a Mac. Disney+ doesn’t offer a downloadable app for macOS, and their download feature is limited to mobile devices. If you really need to download Disney Plus movies on Mac, you can consider the methods mentioned for Netflix.

Part 3. How to Download Amazon Prime Movies on Mac

Unlike Netflix and Disney+, which don’t offer dedicated Mac apps, Amazon Prime gives you the flexibility to download movies and shows directly on your MacBook. Here’s how:

Step 1. Open the Mac App Store and search for “Prime Video”. Then, download and install the app on your Mac.

Step 2. Launch the Prime Video app and browse the extensive library. You can search for specific titles, explore categories, or check out recommendations.

Step 3. Once you’ve found a movie or show you want to download for watching offline, click on it to open the details page. Look for the “Download” button and click it to save the content to your Mac. After that, you can enjoy your favorite Amazon Prime movies anytime, anywhere, even without an internet connection.

Downloaded videos are easily accessible. Click the “Downloads” tab at the bottom of the app to see your saved content. Here, you can manage your downloads, play them offline, or even delete them when you’re finished.

To remove a downloaded movie or show, head to the “Downloads” tab, select the title, and click the three-dot menu. Choose “Delete Download” to confirm the removal.

Part 4. How to Download Movies on MacBook from Apple TV+

If you are an Apple TV subscriber, you can download movies on MacBook from Apple TV+. But not all movies are available for download on Mac. Only movies with a download button is available for offline viewing. Follow the steps below to download movies on Mac from Apple TV+. Downloads are available for up to 30 days before they expire.

Step 1. Open the Apple TV app on your Mac. Find the movie you want to download and click on it to open its details page.

Step 2. Look for the download button (it looks like an arrow pointing downwards) and click on it. The movie will begin downloading. You can check the progress of the download by clicking on the “Downloads” button at the top of the screen. Once the download is complete, you can watch the movie offline by going to the “Library” tab in the Apple TV app and selecting the movie.

Part 11. FAQs about Free Downloading Movies on MacBook

1. How do I download movies to watch offline on my Mac?

You several ways to download movies on your Mac. Here are some options - Amazon Prime Video, Netflix, Google Play, Vudu, FandangoNOW, and more. They require a subscription or rental fee. Ensure that you download movies on Mac from reputable sources for personal use.

2. What services can you download movie on Mac?

There are several legal services that allow you to download movies on a Mac, such as Apple iTunes, Amazon Prime Video, Netflix, Disney+, and more. With a subscription, you can download movies and TV episodes to your Mac using the movie streaming app. The downloads are available for offline viewing.

3. Why won’t my movie download on my Mac?

There could be several reasons why your movie won’t download on your Mac, insufficient storage, internet connection, account issues, compatibility, content availability, app updates, etc. Not all movies may be available for download due to licensing restrictions. Make sure you are logged into the relevant service account (e.g., iTunes, Netflix) and your subscription is active. Ensure that the app you’re using is up-to-date, as outdated apps may have download issues.
