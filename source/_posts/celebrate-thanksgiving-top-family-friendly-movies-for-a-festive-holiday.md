The Grinch is another good movie for Thanksgiving holiday that is suitable for kids. The movie is based on the favorite character of children's book How the Grinch Stole Christmas! Voiced by Benedict Cumberbatch, the animated version just comes right in time for the holiday. Don't hesitate to download the Thanksgiving movie trailer to see if your kids would like it.

For kids who want to roar with laughter while enjoying movies, Planes, Trains and Automobiles is absolutely suitable family entertainment.
