##  How to Use ChatGPT on iPhone or iPad

 To use ChatGPT on an iPhone or iPad, download the [official ChatGPT app for iPhone](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/openai-chatgpt/id6448311069). This app was released on May 18, 2023.

 To use the web-based version of ChatGPT instead, simply visit [chat.openai.com](https://chat.openai.com/) in a browser such as Safari---you can also [add a shortcut to the home screen](https://android-pokemon-go.techidaily.com/unova-stone-pokemon-go-evolution-list-and-how-catch-them-for-nokia-c12-drfone-by-drfone-virtual-android/) for easy launching.

 First, open Safari (this doesn't work with other browsers) and navigate to the [ChatGPT website](https://chat.openai.com/). Tap the share button (up arrow within a square) in the toolbar.

![Tap the share button.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/IMG_0255.png) 

 Scroll through the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen." If you don't see this option, it can be added from the "[Edit Actions](https://screen-mirror.techidaily.com/overview-of-the-best-motorola-moto-e13-screen-mirroring-app-drfone-by-drfone-android/)" option at the bottom of the share sheet.

![Tap "Add to Home Screen."](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_0241.png) 

 Next, change the shortcut name to "ChatGPT" and tap "Add."

![Edit the name and tap "Add."](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/IMG_0257.png) 

 The website shortcut will be added to your home screen! You can also [set up ChatGPT to work through Siri with the Apple Shortcuts app](https://remote-screen-capture.techidaily.com/updated-win10-gaming-sessions-best-recording-approaches/). You can even [use ChatGPT on an Apple Watch](https://extra-resources.techidaily.com/new-capture-clarity-editing-insights-for-professional-results/).
