## Part 3: Extended Knowledge for VOB 

VOB is the abbreviation of Video Object, a container format in DVD media. It contains digital video, audio, subtitles, menus, navigation contents, and info of a DVD movie. 

For unencrypted VOB file, a player of generic MPEG-2 files, such as VLC, MPlayer, etc can deal with VOB playback.   
 But in some cases, VOB files can be copy protected, resulting in difficulty in [playing VOB files](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/). But you can still rely on some trustworthy media player such as [5KPlayer](https://tools.techidaily.com/5kplayer/products/) as it can handle the encrypted DVDs and video files with ease.
