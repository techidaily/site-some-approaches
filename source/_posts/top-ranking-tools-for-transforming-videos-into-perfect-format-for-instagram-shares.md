## Now You're Able to Upload Videos to Instagram!

After you have converted videos to Instagram supported formats, then you can [upload video to Instagram](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) with your mobile phone. Just open the Instagram App and go through the following steps:

1. Tap the Plus icon at the bottom of the screen.
2. Tap "Library" on the bottom left.
3. Select the video from your camera roll and tap Next.

If you find your video is too long for Instagram, tap trim at the bottom to have the video meet the requirements of 60 seconds.
