Originally developed by Macromedia, FLV container file format is supported in Adobe Flash player. Nowadays, FLV video format has become the dominant web video format and everyone can online watch FLV videos from sites like YouTube, Hulu, VEVO, Yahoo! It is worth mentioning that this free FLV player for Mac also enables you to free download HD or UHD FLV videos from online video sharing sites like YouTube, Dailymotion, Vimeo, Vevo, Facebook and More.
