## What Is Watermark and How Does It Work?

A watermark (more specifically speaking, digital watermark) is a recognizable audio, text, picture, or video which is deliberately embedded into a digital image, audio or video file. The watermarks are mainly used to provide the identity of its owners of the media so when the signal is copied, then the digital information also is carried in the copy for providing copyright protection. However, if would really affect your experience if you see unnecessary logo, icon, scroll words appearing over the DVD movies.
